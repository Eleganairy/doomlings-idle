## The Origin Story

### The Meteorite Impact

A meteorite carrying alien life crashed onto Earth. Among the debris were small, strange creatures called **Slimes** - simple organisms that had traveled through space for millennia. These slimes possess a unique ability: they can **absorb and adapt** to the traits of living things around them.

### The Awakening

Upon landing on Earth, the slimes began to feed on the meteorite fragments scattered across the landscape. The meteorite's energy had already begun affecting Earth's ecosystem - plants became sentient, animals grew aggressive, and nature itself turned hostile.

The slimes, feeding on this cosmic energy, started developing new abilities and forms. Each trait they absorb from the Earth's creatures makes them stronger, giving them new powers to survive in this transformed world.

## The Slimes

The playable characters are **Slimes** - alien organisms from the meteorite. As they absorb traits from Earth's creatures, they evolve into specialized forms:

### Basic Slime

_"The basic slime that flew with the meteorite to Earth. It does not look like it can do anything special. Anyway, let's feed it meteorite, that is a good idea."_

The original form. A simple, adaptable creature that serves as the foundation for all evolution.

### Druid Slime

_"Using the energy of living beings and trees, this slime is able to use the energy it gains for good. Instead of dealing damage, this slime can heal its allies and has strong abilities in return for weaker base stats."_

A support-focused evolution that draws power from nature to heal allies.

### Electric Slime

_"Using the electricity from the planet and from the life around it, this slime generates so much energy that it is completely made out of electricity. This slime can attack fast and generate strong attacks that damage all enemies."_

An offensive evolution that harnesses electrical energy for devastating AoE attacks.

### Fortified Slime

_"Scraping the bark of the fortified trees brought to life a tanky slime that can take more damage and shield itself to protect himself from taking damage. Those trees came in handy after all."_

A defensive evolution with high health and shield abilities.

## Core Concepts

### Energy

Energy is the life force collected from defeated enemies. It's used to power upgrades and unlock new abilities.

### Traits

As slimes evolve, they develop **Traits** - permanent abilities unlocked through gameplay achievements. Traits unlock new upgrade paths and represent the slime's adaptation to Earth.

## Themes & Tone

- **Evolution & Adaptation**: The core theme - growing stronger by absorbing traits from enemies
- **Cosmic Horror Lite**: Something alien has landed, and the world is changing
- **Nature Corrupted**: Earth's creatures have become hostile due to the meteorite's influence
- **Idle Progression**: A sense of constant growth even when not actively playing
